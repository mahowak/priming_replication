Scheepers & Sturt (2014), Experiment 1 (language to math priming)

Variables are:

Subject (L2M01-L2M36)

Serial trial-position in experiment (6-75)

list (1-4)

item (1-24)

prime (prime condition): 1=right branching,2=left-branching

target (target condition): 1=right branching,2=left-branching

pres_C (expected correct result for prime): integer

pres_F (expected result for prime given incorrect structuring): integer

presp (prime response): 1-5 sensicality rating

tresp (actual target response): integer

correct: 0=incorrectly solved target, 1=correctly solved target

numerr: whether numerical error is likely (1) or not (0)
