Data from Scheepers et al. (2011, Psych Science), Experiment 1

Variables are:

Subject (1-108)

Group (B=business, P=psychology, M=maths)

Serial trial-position in experiment (7-99)

Item (1-24)

Prime Condition (1=HA, 2=LA, 3=baseline)

PrimeCorrect (0=incorrectly solved, 1=correctly solved)

TargetAttach (1=HA, 2=LA, 3=unclassifiable)
